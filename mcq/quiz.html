🚀 New from Superfile: Tables, Forms & Multimedia

  • Tables: Use <table>, <tr> (row), <th> (header), <td> (data). Use colspan to span multiple columns.
  • Forms: <fieldset> groups related fields, <legend> provides a title for them. <label> should connect to an <input> via for/id.
  • Multimedia: <audio> and <video> need the controls attribute to be usable. <iframe> embeds another webpage.
